## 2024-05-22 - Dynamic Image Accessibility
**Learning:** Dynamic text images (like `readme-typing-svg`) and decorative GIFs are common in profile READMEs but often lack accessible descriptions or pollute the screen reader experience.
**Action:** Always inspect dynamic image URLs for content to populate `alt` text, and explicitly mark flanking header graphics as decorative with `alt=""`.
